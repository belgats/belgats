Modality Influence in Multimodal Machine Learning

Type: Journal paper | Status: In-Progress
Link: arXiv:2306.06476

Description: This study delves into Multimodal Machine Learning, investigating the influence of different modalities in tasks like Sentiment Analysis, Emotion Recognition, and more. It addresses existing gaps by proposing a methodology to assess each modality's impact on various models and datasets across different tasks. By training state-of-the-art models with concealed modalities, the research aims to determine the most influential modality per task. Through this investigation, the study contributes valuable insights into the individual roles of modalities, fostering advancements in multimodal learning.
